About Levante

Independent, senior and commercially minded

Levante Corporate Finance supports founders, shareholders and management teams in moments that require strong financial judgement. That may be a transaction, a growth phase, a financing process or simply the need for a sharper finance function.

In addition to traditional corporate finance work, Levante provides fractional CFO support for companies that need senior financial leadership, but not necessarily on a full-time basis. This includes reporting, planning, cash flow oversight, stakeholder communication and strategic finance projects.
